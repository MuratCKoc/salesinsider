What is ETL?

ETL stands for Etract, Transform and Load. The best way to imagine ETL is as a pipeline. The data (the oil) is etracted from the ground and then, refined through the pipeline (transformed) before finally reaching a consumable end state (loaded).

The Extraction Process

The first step in any sort of data decision making is the Extraction process. This can process can be subdivided into three steps:


  • Identifying the data that you will be using.

  • Validating the data, to ensure that values being provided are in line with with the given domain that you are investigating.

  • Determining the format, that you want your dataset to come in. Some common-source formats are JSON, and flat files (CSVs, etc.).
