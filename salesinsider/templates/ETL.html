What is ETL?

ETL stands for Etract, Transform and Load. The best way to imagine ETL is as a pipeline. The data (the oil) is etracted from the ground and then, refined through the pipeline (transformed) before finally reaching a consumable end state (like plastic).

The Etraction Process

The first step in any sort of data integration strategy is the etraction process. This can process can be subdivided into three steps:


  • Identifying the data that you will be using.

  • Validating the data, to ensure that values being provided are in line with with the given domain that you are investigating.

  • Determining the format, that you want your dataset to come in. Some common-source formats are JSON, and flat files.
